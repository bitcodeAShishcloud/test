🔊 Pronunciation Guide
▸Stress the right syllable: pre-SENT (verb) vs PRE-sent (noun)
▸Link words together: "want to" → "wanna", "going to" → "gonna"
▸Reduce unstressed vowels: "about" → "uh-BOUT"
▸Final consonants matter: "bat" ≠ "bad" ≠ "ban"
